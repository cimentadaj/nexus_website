THE 15-YEAR FINDING
When a city speaks at SCEWC, the topic appears in their own governance documents a median of 5.2 years later.
Across 14 editions, 73% of speaker cities have at least one SCEWC-programmed topic in their current strategic documents. The diffusion is real, measurable, and traceable. SCEWC is not just a marketplace — it is a global agenda-setting infrastructure for urban policy.
MOST AMPLIFIED
Digital Twins moved from research to city plans in 4 years
First appeared in SCEWC papers in 2017. Programmed on the main stage by 2019. Now in the strategic plans of 47 cities globally, including Curitiba, Helsinki, Singapore, and Dubai.
FASTEST RISE
Urban AI Ethics — from zero panels to 38 in two editions
Did not exist as a SCEWC topic before 2023. By 2025 it had its own stage and 38 dedicated panels. Already adopted in formal charters by Seoul, Helsinki, and Amsterdam.
SIGNATURE TOPIC
SCEWC made Superblocks a global urban planning category
Programmed by Barcelona at SCEWC 2016. Tracked into formal plans in Vienna, Buenos Aires, Bogotá, Berlin, and Brisbane by 2024 — every adoption cites Barcelona as the reference.
